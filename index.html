About Me

Welcome to my academic portfolio

I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow specializing in AI-driven optimization and large language models for complex transportation systems at Singapore University of Technology and Design, working with Prof. Ying XU.

My research focuses on urban freight systems, multimodal transport integration, and data-driven transportation analysis and optimization, with particular emphasis on the application of artificial intelligence in transportation. I have a strong background in both transportation engineering and cutting-edge AI technologies.

I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Engineering Systems and Design from SUTD in December 2023, where I was awarded the prestigious President Graduate Fellowship and maintained a GPA of 4.25. My doctoral research investigated the integration of freight and passenger transport systems.
